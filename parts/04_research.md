## Research Experience

### Hybrid Transformer for Multi-Focus Image Fusion (MFIF)
**Manipal University Jaipur** | Jaipur, Rajasthan, India
*May 2025 - August 2025* | **Funded Research Position**

Research on deep learning architecture combining FocalViT and CrossViT transformers for multi-focus image fusion, addressing the challenge of merging images with different focal planes into a single all-in-focus result.

- **Architecture:** Designed hybrid transformer combining FocalViT's hierarchical focal attention with CrossViT's cross-branch interaction for superior spatial-spectral feature extraction
- **Implementation:** PyTorch-based model trained on Lytro multi-focal dataset, deployed on HuggingFace (Model Hub + Spaces) and Kaggle
- **Results:** Achieved state-of-the-art fusion quality on benchmark datasets, demonstrating improved preservation of high-frequency details compared to CNN-based approaches
- **Impact:** Provides practical solution for photography, microscopy, and medical imaging applications requiring extended depth of field
- **Tech Stack:** PyTorch, Transformers, Computer Vision, Gradio, HuggingFace Ecosystem
